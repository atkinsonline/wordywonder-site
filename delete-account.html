Wordy Wonder Account Deletion

Last updated: 15 March 2026

This page explains how to delete your Wordy Wonder account and what data is deleted or retained.

Delete account in the app

  1. Open Wordy Wonder.
  2. Go to Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Type DELETE to confirm.

Deletion is immediate once confirmed.

Data deleted

  • Authentication account record (email/password login)
  • User profile data (including nickname)
  • Associated gameplay/profile-linked records owned by the account
  • Device push token records linked to the account

Data retained

We do not intentionally retain personal account data after account deletion, except where temporary technical logs or backups are required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

Any such retained operational data is kept only for the minimum required period.
